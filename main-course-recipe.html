One-Pot Veg Khichdi

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1 small carrot (chopped)
  • 1 potato (diced)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 inch ginger (grated)
  • 3-4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash rice and dal together until water runs clear. Soak for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions, ginger, and garlic. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  4. Add vegetables and spices. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Drain rice and dal, add to the cooker with water and salt.
  6. Pressure cook for 2 whistles or about 15 minutes.
  7. Let pressure release naturally. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Add more water for a softer consistency
  • Top with ghee and crispy fried onions for extra flavor
  • Serve with papad and pickle for a complete meal
  • Can be made with any seasonal vegetables
