Masala Veg Toast

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (chopped)
  • 1 small capsicum (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp butter or oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix chopped veggies with pepper and chili powder in a bowl.
  2. Spread the mixture over bread slices.
  3. Heat a pan with butter and toast the bread on the veggie side first.
  4. Flip and toast the other side lightly. Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.

Chef's Tips

  • Use day-old bread for better toasting
  • Chop vegetables finely for better distribution
  • Add cheese on top for an extra indulgent version
