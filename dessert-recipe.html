Suji Halwa

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (suji)
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2.5 cups water
  • 1/4 cup mixed nuts (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Few strands of saffron (optional)
  • Raisins for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add semolina and roast until light golden and fragrant (about 8-10 minutes).
  3. Meanwhile, in another pan, heat water and add sugar. Stir until dissolved.
  4. Add cardamom powder and saffron to the sugar syrup.
  5. Carefully add the hot sugar syrup to the roasted semolina.
  6. Stir continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  7. Add chopped nuts and mix well.
  8. Garnish with raisins and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Roast the semolina on medium-low heat for best results
  • The sugar syrup should be hot when added to avoid lumps
  • Add a pinch of saffron for a beautiful aroma and color
  • Can be stored in an airtight container for 2-3 days
